Our story

When every piece
becomes a work of art.

Inspired by kintsugi, the Japanese art of mending pottery with gold, our restaurant celebrates the idea that beauty is born from detail and patience.

Each sushi is shaped by hand, in front of you, by a chef trained in Tokyo. Every grain of rice is counted, every slice of fish chosen for its extreme freshness.

An experience designed for those who seek more than a meal: a moment suspended in time.

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know before your visit

Do you welcome children?

Yes, children are welcome. A high chair is available on request, and our chef can adapt some creations for younger palates.

Do you have vegetarian or vegan options?

Absolutely. Our "Chef's Garden" roll is 100% vegetarian, and several creations can be adapted as vegan. Let us know your preferences when booking.

What is your dress code?

Smart casual recommended. No sportswear or shorts. We aim to preserve the refined atmosphere of the restaurant.

How do you handle allergies?

All allergens are listed on request. Mention your allergies when booking: our chef will adapt your menu accordingly.

What is your cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before the reservation. After that, a $25/person fee may apply for groups of 6+.

Is there parking nearby?

Paid street parking (free after 9 PM). Indigo parking lot 2 minutes away on foot. Sherbrooke metro (orange line) is 5 minutes away.

Is the restaurant accessible for people with reduced mobility?

Yes, the entrance is on ground level and our restrooms are wheelchair accessible. Please let us know in advance to reserve a suitable table.

Do you host private events?

Yes, private bookings available from 20 people. Birthdays, corporate events, engagements: contact us for a custom quote.
